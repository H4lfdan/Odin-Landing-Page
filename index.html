Some random information.

Goats have silly little outward turned horns that get their heads stuck in fences a lot.
Goats' small, pointy hooves allow them to walk up nearly veritcal surfaces.
I couldn't tell you why some goats have beards. Sometimes they look cool as hell, other times they just look doofy.
Goats are evolved to jump around like little maniacs. This is probably why English speaking humans call their own offspring "kids".
